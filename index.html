Overview

Developed a comprehensive Bus Booking System aimed at simplifying the process of booking bus tickets for passengers. The system offers an intuitive web-based platform that enables users to search for bus routes, compare prices, and book seats online in real-time. It integrates advanced features like e-ticket generation, seat selection, and payment processing, providing a seamless and efficient booking experience.
